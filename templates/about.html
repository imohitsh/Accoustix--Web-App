What is Accoustix?

Accoustix is a fast, simple, secure, and fun way to share the moment with your friends and family. Accoustix provides secure messaging and calling in a single go!

How we do what we do?

With our end-to-end encryption, you can safely connect with your loved ones. When end-to-end encrypted, your messages and calls are secured so only you and the person you're communicating with can read or listen to them, and nobody in between, not even us.
